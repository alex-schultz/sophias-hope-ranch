About Sophia's Hope Ranch

Sophia’s Hope Ranch is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to healing families impacted by attachment disruptions and trauma. We foster secure attachments through faith in Jesus, the ultimate Healer, and employ innovative, evidence-based practices to promote connection, health, and well-being.

Our Mission

At Sophia's Hope Ranch, we believe that healing happens when families are supported with compassionate, faith-centered care. Our mission is to provide comprehensive treatment for attachment disruption and trauma, helping families rebuild trust, connection, and hope.

Founder: Theresa Schultz

Founder: Theresa Schultz

I'm Theresa Schultz, Christ-follower, mother, and clinical therapist. With five grown children (two adopted, three biological) and five amazing grandchildren, my heart has always been drawn to caring for children and families in need. For over 30 years, I've served in many roles - foster mom, CASA volunteer, board member, and therapist, each one deepening my passion for helping children heal. As a trauma and attachment specialist, and as a mom to a daughter who experienced attachment disruption, I’ve witnessed the heartbreaking struggle to build safe, connected relationships. Sophia’s Hope Ranch was born from both the pain and the hope of that journey, a reflection of my lifelong calling to help families heal.

Our Services

Comprehensive care tailored to your family's unique needs

Trauma Recovery

Comprehensive trauma-informed care using proven therapeutic methods to promote healing and resilience.

Individual Therapy

Tailored sessions for personal healing and growth. Available for the child, parents, and siblings.

Family Therapy

Strengthening family bonds and addressing collective challenges through personalized therapy sessions.

Marriage Counseling

Reviving intimacy, building teamwork, and realizing your family's vision.

Faith-Based Counseling

Integrating spiritual care with clinical expertise to provide holistic healing for mind, body, and spirit.

Residential Care

Safe, nurturing residential environment where families can focus on healing in a supportive community.

Treatment Programs

Structured programs designed for lasting transformation

Intensive Outpatient Program

For children ages 2 and up, offering comprehensive support to address attachment and trauma while allowing them to remain in their home environment.

  • Individual and family therapy sessions
  • Parenting skills training
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Spiritual guidance and support

Inpatient Program

For children ages 5-17, providing immersive, 24/7 care in a supportive, therapeutic environment. Focuses on deep healing and personal growth, helping children navigate their challenges while engaging in a structured, faith-centered recovery process.

  • Daily therapy sessions
  • Group therapy programs
  • Crisis intervention support
  • Equine Therapy
