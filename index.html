About Sophia's Hope Ranch

Sophia’s Hope Ranch is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to healing families impacted by attachment disruptions and trauma. We foster secure attachments through faith in Jesus, the ultimate Healer, and employ innovative, evidence-based practices to promote connection, health, and well-being.

Our Mission

At Sophia's Hope Ranch, we believe that healing happens when families are supported with compassionate, faith-centered care. Our mission is to provide comprehensive treatment for attachment disruption and trauma, helping families rebuild trust, connection, and hope.

Board of Directors

"For over 30 years, I've served in many roles - foster mom, CASA volunteer, board member, and therapist, each one deepening my passion for helping children heal. Our Services

Comprehensive care tailored to your family's unique needs. Our mission is to transform families affected by attachment disruptions through faith-based, individualized care. Recognizing that therapy isn’t one-size-fits-all, we employ diverse, evidence-based practices to nurture healing, connection, and well-being.

Trauma Recovery

Trust-Based Relational Intervention (TBRI) for vulnerable children who have experienced adversity, toxic stress, or trauma. Employs evidence-based strategies to build trust and foster healthy relationships.

Individual Therapy

Tailored sessions for personal healing and growth; available for children, parents, and siblings.

Family Therapy

Strengthening family bonds and addressing collective challenges through personalized therapy sessions.

Couples Therapy

Reviving intimacy, building teamwork, and realizing your family's vision. Strong focus on communication and recognizing and understanding each other's perspective.

Equine Therapy

Leverage unique sensitivity of horses to facilitate emotional, mental, and physical well-being. Strengthens trust and emotional resilience via human-horse interactions.

Play Therapy

Use children's natural medium of expression to help them communicate and resolve emotional or behavioral issues. Facilitates emotional expression and healing through guided play activities.

Expressive Arts Therapy

Integrates multiple artistic modalities, including visual arts, music, dance, drama, poetry, and storytelling to promote growth, development, and healing.

Workshop: Breaking General Curses

Addressing and overcoming patterns crossed through generations. We evaluate environmental and spiritual influences to break free of generational strongholds.

Workshop: Parenting

Learn and develop new strategies for parenting. Connecting, empowering, and correcting are the main pillars for this workshop.

Treatment Programs

Families are our clients; not just an individual. Our structured programs are designed for lasting transformation, so individualized treatment plans are developed for all members of the family.

Intensive Outpatient Program

For children ages 2 and up, offering comprehensive support to address attachment and trauma while allowing them to remain in their home environment.

  • Individual and family therapy sessions
  • Parenting skills training
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Spiritual guidance and support

Inpatient Program

For children ages 5-17, providing immersive, 24/7 care in a supportive, therapeutic environment. Focuses on deep healing and personal growth, helping children navigate their challenges while engaging in a structured, faith-centered recovery process.

  • Daily therapy sessions
  • Group therapy programs
  • Crisis intervention support
  • Equine Therapy
