Sophia's Hope Ranch

There are over 60,000 failed adoptions each year. Many hopeful and well-meaning families set out to grow their families through adoption. Unfortunately, they are not prepared for the big feelings and behaviors that come from attachment disruption and return the child back into the system.

Regardless of the way the adoption occurred, every adoption begins with loss and grief. Due to this loss and grief, the child may have difficulty forming emotional attachments with their new caregivers, have extreme aggression, power struggles, defiance, or withdrawal. Attachment disruption affects the adopted child’s emotional, behavioral, cognitive, social, and overall well-being, as well as the well-being of the entire family. That is why the family is the client, not only the child.

About Us

At Sophia's Hope Ranch, we believe in the power of faith and connection to heal families affected by attachment disruption and trauma. As a Christ-centered ministry, our mission is to facilitate attachment to Jesus as the ultimate Healer and source of secure attachment. Our interdisciplinary approach combines innovative, evidence-based practices to inspire connection, health, and well-being.

Mission Statement

As a Christ-centered ministry, Sophia's Hope Ranch will facilitate attachment to Jesus as the ultimate Healer and source of secure attachment. We are dedicated to transforming families affected by attachment disruption through an interdisciplinary treatment approach. Sophia's Hope Ranch recognizes that therapy is not a one size fits all and will utilize various innovative, evidence-based practices to inspire connection, health, and well-being in each family served.

Vision Statement

We aim to see families and children create meaningful and sustainable attachments to establish a positive future outlook.

Our Approach Includes

  • Play Therapy: Encouraging emotional expression and healing through structured play.
  • Equine Therapy: Utilizing the bond between horses and humans to build trust and emotional resilience.
  • Expressive Art Therapy: Facilitating personal exploration and healing through creative expression.
  • Trust-Based Relational Intervention (TBRI): Evidence-based practices focusing on building trust and healthy relationships.

My Story

Welcome to the heart of Sophia’s Hope Ranch, a place born from a profound personal journey and a deep-seated commitment to transforming the lives of children and families. My name is Theresa Schultz, and my path as a devoted Christ-follower, mother, and clinical therapist has led me to this pivotal moment. With five grown children - two adopted, three biological - and three wonderful grandchildren, my life has been a tapestry woven with love, advocacy, and unyielding dedication to nurturing children in need.

For over two decades, I have channeled my passion for advocating for children into various roles, from serving on numerous boards to fostering over a dozen children and dedicating 12 years as a CASA. My professional journey as a clinical therapist specializing in trauma and attachment has been both challenging and rewarding, shaping my understanding of the profound needs of children and families navigating their own struggles.

The inception of Sophia’s Hope Ranch is deeply personal. When I first adopted my daughter at nine months old, her struggles with Reactive Attachment Disorder were heartbreaking and isolating. I felt the weight of her pain and the absence of sufficient resources to support her effectively. Determined to change this reality, I delved into research and sought out every possible avenue for support. The journey was arduous, but it ignited a fire within me to ensure that no other family would face such trials alone.

With the passage of time and the accumulation of resources, I am finally able to bring this dream to fruition. Sophia’s Hope Ranch stands as a testament to the belief that even amidst our deepest struggles, God’s grace and redemption can turn pain into purpose. We are committed to offering healing, support, and comfort to those who need it most, guided by the conviction that what was once meant for harm can be transformed into something profoundly good.

Join us as we embark on this mission to create a sanctuary of hope and healing, where every child and family can find solace, strength, and the unwavering support they deserve.

Our Programs

  • Individual Therapy: Tailored sessions for personal healing and growth for the child, parents, and siblings.
  • Family Therapy: Strengthening family bonds and addressing collective challenges through personalized therapy sessions.
  • Intensive Outpatient Program: For children ages 2 and up, offering comprehensive support to address attachment and trauma while allowing them to remain in their home environment.
  • Residential Program: For children ages 5–17, providing immersive, 24/7 care in a supportive, therapeutic environment. Our residential program focuses on deep healing and personal growth, helping children navigate their challenges while engaging in a structured, faith-centered recovery process.

Workshops

  • Breaking Generational Curses: Addressing and overcoming patterns crossed through generations.
  • Taking Spiritual Authority Over the Household: Empowering families to establish spiritual leadership and harmony.
  • Parenting: Developing new strategies to parenting.
  • Marriage: Reviving intimacy, building teamwork, and realizing your family's vision.
  • And More: Customized workshops based on your family's needs.

Our Pillars of Healing

  • Faith: Trusting in Jesus as the source of true healing.
  • Connection: Fostering strong, supportive relationships.
  • Surrender: Embracing God’s plan and letting go of past hurts.
  • Responsibility: Encouraging personal and collective accountability for growth and healing.
